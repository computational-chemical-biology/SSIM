QUERY scaninfo(MS2DATA) WHERE MSPROD=227.0

'add 10ppm tolerance'

QUERY scaninfo(MS2DATA) WHERE MS2PROD=227.0:TOLERANCEPPM=10

please also look for 229 m/z

QUERY scaninfo(MS2DATA) WHERE MS2PROD=227.0:TOLERANCEPPM=10 AND MS2PROD=229.0:TOLERANCEPPM=10

format this better

QUERY scaninfo(MS2DATA) WHERE
